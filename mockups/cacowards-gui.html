Cacowards GUI mockups two takes on the same data shape

Same caco chrome (sidebar, top bar, status bar). Differences are confined to the central panel and how the Cacowards surface signals that it's a curated annual feature pulled from Doomworld vs. just another database listing. Both wire to the existing caco enrich --cacowards --year YYYY pipeline and use the same c.YEAR.cat.RANK display ids.

Mockup A — Magazine

A year banner, year-strip nav, and category sections of poster cards. Most "this is a thing from outside, dressed up" — leans into the editorial character.
